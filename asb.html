What is ASB?¿Qué es el ASB?

The Associated Student Body (ASB) is the official student government of Interlake High School. Elected by the student body, ASB officers work to represent all students, plan and run school events, promote school spirit, and serve as the primary link between students and school administration.

El Gobierno Estudiantil (ASB) es el gobierno estudiantil oficial de Interlake High School. Elegidos por los estudiantes, los oficiales del ASB trabajan para representar a todos los alumnos, organizar eventos escolares, promover el espíritu escolar y ser el enlace principal entre los estudiantes y la administración.

ASB oversees major school traditions including Homecoming, Spirit Week, dances, pep assemblies, and community service initiatives. Officers meet regularly to manage the student activities budget and coordinate with clubs, classes, and staff to make Interlake the best it can be.

El ASB supervisa las principales tradiciones escolares como el Homecoming, la Semana del Espíritu, bailes, asambleas y proyectos de servicio comunitario. Los oficiales se reúnen regularmente para gestionar el presupuesto de actividades estudiantiles y coordinarse con clubes, clases y personal.
