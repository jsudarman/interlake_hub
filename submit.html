Guidelines for Club Officers

  • Agendas — submit before the meeting. Helps members plan and lets absent students know what's covered.
  • Recaps — submit within 2 days after a meeting. Include highlights, decisions, and action items.
  • Announcements — anytime for deadlines, competition results, new members, or other club news.
  • Keep content clear. Remember: this is read by students considering joining your club.
